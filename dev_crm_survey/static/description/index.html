CRM Lead Pipeline Survey in odoo

CRM Lead Pipeline Survey odoo application will add Survey on the CRM so you can take detailed feedback from the customer about their issue. You can add multiple survey on the CRM.

More Apps Ask for Demo

FEATURES

  • Configure Survey Template and add multiple survey into the template.
  • Just select survey template on the CRM and all the survey will link automatically into the CRM.
  • If customer is logged in and viewing the CRM then he/she can directly start the survey from the CRM screen and give answers .
  • If user rather than customer is logged in then he/she can send survey to the customer via email. So customer can start survey from email link and answer the survey.
  • Answer of the survey is automatically linked with the CRM once customer complete the survey.
  • Answer will always link with the CRM, either customer start survey directly from the CRM or start survey from the email link.
  • You can easily print Last Answer of the survey from the CRM, because latest answer will always linked with the survey on the CRM.
